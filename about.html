About Us

101 Bagels is available in-store, online, and for delivery

101 Bagels in Oceanside, CA is a bakery and cafe that features a complete, online menu for breakfast and lunch. Our shop features smoothies and local, fresh products that are fast and cheap

Located right in the heart of downtown Oceanside, 101 Bagels is the perfect stop every time you’re in the area. Whether you’re headed to the beach, grabbing your morning pick-me-up, or just walking through the city, our fresh bagels, coffee, and breakfast hits make the perfect fuel. Stop by each time you’re in downtown — 101 Bagels is your must-visit Oceanside classic.
